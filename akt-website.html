Who We Are

A Trust Built on Compassion & Integrity

Ansaar Ul Khair Trust (AKT) is a registered non-governmental organization dedicated to serving the most vulnerable members of Pakistani society. Founded on the principles of humanity, dignity, and justice, we work tirelessly to uplift underprivileged families, educate young minds, and foster community growth.

Our work is guided by a firm commitment to transparency — every rupee donated reaches those who need it most, and we publish detailed accounts of our activities and finances.

About AKT

Who We Are

A people-driven non-profit committed to uplifting the underprivileged through transparent, compassionate action.

Our Story

Founded on Faith & Humanity

Ansaar Ul Khair Trust (AKT) was established with a singular purpose: to serve those who have been left behind by society. Inspired by the Islamic principle of looking after one's neighbours and community, our founders came together to create a structured, transparent, and impactful organization.

Since our founding, we have worked across multiple cities and districts, partnering with local volunteers, regional NGOs, and international donors to deliver essential services to thousands of beneficiaries.

We believe that charity must be paired with dignity — every individual we serve is treated with the utmost respect and compassion.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide welfare services, education, and human rights advocacy to the most vulnerable segments of Pakistani society — with full transparency and community involvement.

Our Vision

A Pakistan where every citizen, regardless of their economic status, has access to basic necessities, education, and the opportunity to live a dignified life.

Our Values

Compassion, Integrity, Transparency, Community Empowerment, and Dignity — these are the pillars that guide every decision and action at AKT.

Accountability

Our Transparency Commitment

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of financial and operational transparency.

Annual Reports

We publish detailed annual reports showing all income received, expenses incurred, and beneficiaries served — accessible to all donors and the public.

Independent Audit

Our accounts are independently audited by a certified chartered accountant firm each financial year to ensure accuracy and integrity.

Board Oversight

A diverse board of trustees oversees all fund allocations, ensuring every decision aligns with our mission and serves the best interests of our beneficiaries.

What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive welfare, education, and collaboration programs for a better Pakistan.

Public Wing

Support for Families & Communities

Direct welfare services delivered to those in greatest need across Pakistan.

Food & Ration Support

Monthly food packages delivered to registered poor families, ensuring no household goes hungry.

Financial Assistance

Emergency financial aid to families facing crises such as medical emergencies, natural disasters, or job loss.

Joint Marriages

Dignified mass wedding ceremonies for underprivileged girls, covering all wedding expenses with full support.

Medical Support

Assisting families with medical bills, medicine procurement, and hospital access for those who cannot afford care.

Clothing Drives

Seasonal clothing collections and distributions to underprivileged children and adults across communities.

Legal & Human Rights

Free legal guidance and advocacy for marginalized community members facing discrimination or injustice.

Educational Wing

Investing in Pakistan's Future

Education is the most powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty.

Merit Scholarships

Full and partial scholarships awarded to high-achieving students from low-income backgrounds at secondary and university levels.

Financial Aid

Emergency financial grants covering tuition, books, uniforms, and transport for students at risk of dropping out.

Student Guidance

Career counselling, study mentoring, and academic guidance sessions connecting students with qualified professionals.

Skill Development

Vocational training in trades such as IT, tailoring, carpentry, and electricals to enable self-employment.

Digital Literacy

Free computer and internet literacy programs equipping youth with modern digital skills for the job market.

Girls' Education

Dedicated programs encouraging and financially supporting girls' education in conservative and rural communities.

NGO Collaboration

Together We Reach Further

Partnerships amplify our impact in remote and underserved regions.

NGO Partnerships

Formal partnership agreements with regional NGOs to deliver programs in areas beyond our direct operational reach.

Remote Area Programs

Special initiatives targeting underdeveloped districts in Balochistan, Southern Punjab, and tribal areas.

International Donor Network

Connecting with overseas Pakistanis and international philanthropists to fund nationwide programs.

Joint Projects

Co-designing and co-funding welfare and education projects with partner organizations for maximum community benefit.

Community Development

Long-term community development initiatives including water access, sanitation, and infrastructure support.

Awareness Campaigns

Nationwide awareness drives about education, hygiene, rights, and welfare services available to communities.
